# Sewacity Technologies

### Your city at your fingertips.

India's Tier 2 & Tier 3 super app — bringing food delivery, local services, and everyday city conveniences to India's growing cities, one platform at a time.

---

## Who We Are

Registered in Patna, Bihar in 2021 and now operating out of Bangalore, Sewacity exists because India's smaller cities have been left behind by metro-first apps. While Tier 1 cities enjoy instant delivery and service booking, Tier 2 and Tier 3 India has had to wait. We're closing that gap.

Sewacity brings together food ordering, local service bookings, hotel stays, event tickets, and business listings — all inside one app built specifically for how these cities live and work.

[Visit sewacity.com](https://www.sewacity.com) · [Get the app on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.sewacity.sewacity)

---

## What You Can Do on Sewacity

**For Users**
- Order food from local restaurants with real-time delivery tracking
- Book salons, doctors, tutors, and home services in a few taps
- Reserve hotel rooms and buy tickets to local events

**For Businesses**
- List your service or shop for free, no website needed
- Reach thousands of customers in your own city
- Manage bookings and orders directly through the app
